Our Mission

With 30 years in IT and cybersecurity, Peregrine Cyber helps small businesses cut through the noise. We know how overwhelming tools, vendors, cyber‑insurance requirements, and regulations can be—so we meet you where you are and make sense of it all.

Using proven frameworks such as NIST CSF (National Institute of Standards and Technology Cybersecurity Framework) and CIS Controls (Center for Internet Security), we translate best‑practice standards into plain English and practical, bite‑sized actions that match your budget and risk appetite.

With Peregrine Cyber, you gain clarity, confidence, and an actionable roadmap—security that fits your business, not the other way around.

Cybersecurity Support Packages

Our packages provide ongoing support to build and manage a NIST‑ and CIS‑aligned security program, with clear milestones to guide your journey. Choose the level that fits your needs—each includes recurring deliverables tailored to your business.

Building a security program is an ongoing process, not a one‑time fix—progress depends on your team’s readiness and unique needs. We’ll work with your IT, MSP, or trusted partners to implement technical controls, ensuring a seamless integration. If we’re consistently exceeding your hours, we’ll discuss adjusting your plan to ensure the best support.

  • Walkthrough of critical risk categories
  • Initial gap identification & recommendations
  • Guidance on immediate quick wins
  • Roadmap toward Bronze / Silver / Gold support

Outcome: “We know where we stand and what’s next.”
