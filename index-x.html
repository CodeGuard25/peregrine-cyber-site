Our Mission

With over 30 years in IT and cybersecurity, we know how overwhelming security can feel—especially for small businesses without dedicated tech staff. Maybe you’re relying on one person, a managed service provider (MSP), or handling things yourself, and sorting through tools, vendors, and regulations like HIPAA—or whatever applies to your business—feels more confusing than helpful.

That’s where we come in. We’re your co-pilot, helping you see what’s important, what needs protection—whether that’s patient data, customer information, or your operations—and how to build a security program that fits your business. Using proven frameworks like NIST and CIS, we turn complex standards into plain English and practical steps tailored to your needs.

With Peregrine Cyber, you get a clear roadmap to security—built on trust, clarity, and partnership.

Milestone-Based Cybersecurity Packages

Our milestone-based packages provide a clear path to building a NIST and CIS-aligned security program. Each step evolves with your business, tailored to your size and needs.

✨ Intro → 🔰 Foundation → 🛡️ Defense → 🏁 Resilience

✨ Introductory Assessment

Get a baseline view of your cybersecurity posture

  • 2-hour consultation + templated findings report
  • Quick scan of key risks and current setup
  • Evaluation of a few NIST CSF 2.0 categories
  • Fit recommendation for next steps

A fast, approachable first step—ideal for getting clarity and gauging what level of support you may need.

🔰 Foundation

Milestone: Establish Core Security Hygiene

  • Baseline risk assessment + asset inventory
  • Password and access control review
  • Endpoint protection & backup check
  • Starter security policies
  • Intro-level employee awareness training

Included as part of: Bronze Package (10 hrs/month)

Outcome: “We know what we have, who has access, and we’ve plugged the obvious holes.”

🛡️ Defense

Milestone: Expand Protections and Detection

  • MFA implementation + phishing simulation
  • Email security hardening (e.g., SPF/DKIM)
  • Basic monitoring setup (e.g., log reviews)
  • Incident response plan (starter)
  • Expanded employee training (e.g., social engineering)

Included as part of: Silver Package (16 hrs/month)

Outcome: “We’ve added layers to stop threats and can spot issues early.”

🏁 Resilience

Milestone: Mature and Sustain Your Security Program

  • Full security program documentation (policies + procedures)
  • Compliance mapping (HIPAA, FTC, etc.) + audit prep
  • Incident response drills + recovery planning
  • Ongoing risk reporting for leadership
  • Vendor coordination for advanced testing (e.g., pen tests)

Included as part of: Gold Package (24 hrs/month)

Outcome: “Our security program is mature, auditable, and built to last.”
