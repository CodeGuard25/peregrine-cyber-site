Our Mission

With over 30 years in IT and cybersecurity, we know how overwhelming security can feel for small businesses—especially without a dedicated tech team. You might rely on one person, an MSP, or handle it yourself, but sorting through tools, vendors, regulations, cyber‑insurance requirements—or whatever applies—feels more confusing than helpful.

That’s where we come in. As your cybersecurity advisor, we partner with you to build and manage a security program that fits your business. Using NIST and CIS frameworks, we turn complex standards into plain English, delivering practical steps to protect your customer data or operations. We follow a “trust but verify” approach to catch what might be missed.

With Peregrine Cyber, you get a trusted co‑pilot — guiding you with clarity, confidence, and a roadmap to lasting security.

Cybersecurity Support Packages

Our packages provide ongoing support to build and manage a NIST‑ and CIS‑aligned security program, with clear milestones to guide your journey.

Building a security program is an ongoing process, not a one‑time fix—progress depends on your team’s readiness and goals.

Milestone: Get a Snapshot of Current Risks

A 2‑hour consultation that includes a high‑level walkthrough of your security posture using simplified NIST CSF 2.0 categories and CIS benchmarks. We’ll highlight key gaps and recommend next steps.

  • Walkthrough of critical risk categories
  • Initial gap identification & recommendations
  • Roadmap toward Bronze / Silver / Gold support

Outcome: “We know where we stand and what’s next.”
