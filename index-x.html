Our Mission

With over 30 years in IT and cybersecurity, we know how overwhelming security can feel—especially for small businesses without a dedicated tech staff. Maybe you’re relying on one person, a managed service provider (MSP), or handling things yourself, and sorting through tools, vendors, and regulations like HIPAA—or whatever applies to your business—feels more confusing than helpful.

That’s where we come in. We’re your co‑pilot, helping you see what’s important, what needs protection—whether that’s patient data, customer information, or your operations—and how to build a security program that fits your business. We use proven security frameworks like the National Institute of Standards & Technology (NIST) and the Center for Internet Security (CIS)—but we turn them into plain English and practical steps.

No tool overload or jargon here. You get clear assessments, realistic recommendations, and a steady partner who coordinates with your team and trusted vendors to make it happen. We follow a “trust but verify” approach to ensure nothing slips through the cracks.

With Peregrine Cyber, you get more than advice—you get clarity, confidence, and a security program built for your business.

Cybersecurity Support Packages

Our packages provide ongoing support to build and manage a NIST‑ and CIS‑aligned security program, with clear milestones to guide your journey. Choose the level that fits your needs—each includes recurring deliverables tailored to your business.

Building a security program is an ongoing process, not a one‑time fix—progress depends on your team’s readiness and unique needs. We’ll work with your IT, MSP, or trusted partners to implement technical controls, ensuring a seamless integration. If we’re consistently exceeding your hours, we’ll discuss adjusting your plan to ensure the best support.

📋 Introductory Assessment

Milestone: Get a Snapshot of Current Risks

A 2‑hour consultation that includes a high‑level walkthrough of your security posture using simplified NIST CSF 2.0 categories and CIS benchmarks. We’ll help identify key gaps, answer questions, and recommend next steps.

  • Walkthrough of critical risk categories
  • Initial gap identification (based on discussion)
  • Basic recommendations and guidance
  • Recommendations for Bronze / Silver / Gold support

Outcome: “We know where we stand and what’s next.”
