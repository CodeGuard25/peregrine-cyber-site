Our Mission

With over 30 years in IT and cybersecurity, we know how overwhelming security can feel for small businesses—especially without a dedicated tech team. You might rely on one person, an MSP, or handle it yourself, but sorting through tools, vendors, cyber insurance requirements—or whatever applies—can feel more confusing than helpful.

That’s where we come in. As your cybersecurity advisor, we help you make sense of the chaos—translating industry frameworks like the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) and the Center for Internet Security (CIS) into everyday guidance. Together, we’ll build a security program that works for your business, your budget, and your risk appetite.

With Peregrine Cyber, you gain clarity, confidence, and an actionable roadmap—without the jargon.

Cybersecurity Maturity Path

Our packages provide ongoing support to build and manage a NIST and CIS-aligned security program, with clear milestones to guide your journey. Choose the level that fits your needs—each includes deliverables tailored to your business.

Snapshot Package: 2-Hour Introductory Assessment

Milestone: Get a high-level snapshot of your current security posture

Duration: 2 hours, one-time engagement

What’s Included:

  • A guided walkthrough of your key risk categories using simplified NIST CSF 2.0 and CIS benchmarks
  • Quick-hit gap identification based on your answers and existing tools/processes
  • Plain-English recommendations for next steps (e.g. whether to move into Basic Hygiene, Advanced Protection or Sustained Resilience)
  • A brief list of prioritized actions you can tackle immediately

Basic Hygiene

Milestone: Establish Basic Hygiene

  • Initial risk identification and priority setting
  • Review of core tools (e.g., endpoint protection, firewall basics)
  • Backup configuration guidance
  • Basic access and credential review
  • Review of acceptable use and password policies
  • Monthly vulnerability reviews
  • Support for coordination with IT/MSP as needed

Aligns with CIS IG1 and NIST CSF Identify & Protect.

Advanced Protection

Milestone: Strengthen Protection and Detection

  • Includes Basic Hygiene plus:
  • SIEM/log monitoring guidance (starter)
  • Phishing awareness planning
  • Access management policy planning
  • Email security audit
  • Vendor risk management support
  • Security reporting for internal use

Aligns with CIS IG2 and NIST CSF Detect & Respond.

Sustained Resilience

Milestone: Mature and Sustain Your Security Program

  • Includes Basic Hygiene + Advanced Protection plus:
  • Support for written security policies
  • Compliance mapping (PHI & PII data, FTC, etc.)
  • Incident response tabletop (annual)
  • Testing coordination (e.g., pentest, phishing)
  • Executive risk reporting
  • Recurring policy and control review

Aligns with CIS IG3 and NIST CSF Respond & Recover.
