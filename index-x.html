Our Mission

With 30 years in IT and cybersecurity, Peregrine Cyber helps small businesses cut through the noise. We know how overwhelming tools, vendors, and ever‑changing rules can feel—so we meet you where you are and make sense of it all.

Using the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) Cybersecurity Framework and the Center for Internet Security (CIS) Controls as our compass, we translate best practices into plain English and practical, bite‑sized actions that match your budget and risk. No vendor hype—just a “trust‑but‑verify” partner who coordinates with your IT or MSP so improvements stick.

With Peregrine Cyber you gain clarity, confidence, and an actionable roadmap—security that fits your business, not the other way around.
