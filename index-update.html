Our Mission

With over 30 years in IT and cybersecurity, we know how overwhelming security can feel—especially for small businesses without dedicated tech staff. Maybe you’re relying on one person, a managed service provider (MSP), or handling things yourself, and sorting through tools, vendors, and regulations like HIPAA—or whatever applies to your business—feels more confusing than helpful.

That’s where we come in. We’re your co-pilot, helping you see what’s important, what needs protection—whether that’s patient data, customer information, or your operations—and how to build a security program that fits your business. We use proven security frameworks like the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) and the Center for Internet Security (CIS)—but we turn them into plain English and practical steps.

No tool overload or jargon here. You get clear assessments, realistic recommendations, and a steady partner who coordinates with your team and trusted vendors to make it happen.

With Peregrine Cyber, you get more than advice—you get clarity, confidence, and a security program built for your business.

Milestone-Based Cybersecurity Packages

Our milestone-based packages provide a clear path to improving your security posture. Each one builds on the last — designed to evolve with your business while remaining approachable and flexible.

These packages can be delivered as standalone engagements or included as part of an ongoing monthly support plan. Each one is outcome-focused and built around what matters most: protecting your business.

🔰 Foundation

Milestone: Establish Core Security Hygiene

  • Baseline risk assessment + asset inventory
  • Password and access control review
  • Endpoint protection & backup check
  • Starter security policies
  • Intro-level employee awareness training

Outcome: “We know what we have, who has access, and we’ve plugged the obvious holes.”

🛡️ Defense

Milestone: Build Layers of Protection

  • MFA planning + phishing simulation
  • Starter SIEM or event log review
  • Vendor risk and email filtering audit
  • Incident response plan (starter)
  • Follow-up employee training

Outcome: “We’ve hardened our systems and trained our people to spot and stop threats early.”

🏁 Resilience

Milestone: Auditable, Ongoing Security Program

  • Full written security program (policies + procedures)
  • Internal audit cadence + compliance mapping (HIPAA, FTC, etc.)
  • Tabletop incident drills
  • Executive-level risk reporting
  • Penetration test coordination (optional)

Outcome: “We’re managing security like a business function — with visibility, accountability, and readiness.”
